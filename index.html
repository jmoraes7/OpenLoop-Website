Welcome to OpenLoop

OpenLoop is an alliance of teams at Cornell University, Princeton University, Harvey Mudd College, University of Michigan, Memorial University of Newfoundland, and Northeastern University competing in the Hyperloop competition hosted by SpaceX.

Our goal is the advancement of the Hyperloop technology.
