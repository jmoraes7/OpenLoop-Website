Welcome to OpenLoop

OpenLoop is an alliance of teams at six top universities developing a single entry in this competition. Our goal is to accelerate the development of the Hyperloop and build a case for the construction of a nationwide network.

        We have over 100 students at Cornell University, Harvey Mudd College, University of Michigan, Northeastern University, Memorial University of Newfoundland and Princeton University developing both our prototype pod design and a detailed scalability model for a nationwide network rollout.
